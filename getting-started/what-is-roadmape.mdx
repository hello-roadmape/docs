Welcome to Roadmape Docs. Roadmape helps you to create roadmaps and track alignment between your strategy and execution.

Follow the instructions the customize your workflow and creating your first roadmap.

# 1. Set up your hierarchy
<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Go to workspace settings and click hierarchy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Create your own hierarchy by creating item types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Drag and drop new item types to hierarchy to use them in your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>More detailed information about the hierarchy can be found on the [Hierarchy](/understanding-items/hierarhy).</Info>

# 2. Customize your item types
<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Go to workspace settings and click item types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Create and edit properties for each item type according your product management structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Customize the workflow for each item type according your product development strategy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>More detailed information about the item types can be found on the [Item Types](/understanding-items/item-types).</Info>

# 3. Import your items or set up an integration with your delivery tool
<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Import items" icon="file-csv">
    You can import items from other apps into your Roadmape workspace easily. A .csv file containing your items is all you need.

    More detailed information about the CSV Import can be found on the [CSV Import](/getting-started/csv-import).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Integrate your delivery tool">
    You can integrate Jira or Linear wit Roadmape easily. These integrations enables you to stay update always.

    More detailed information about the Jira Integration can be found on the [Jira Integration](/integrations/jira-integration).

    More detailed information about the Linear Integration can be found on the [Linear Integration](/integrations/linear-integration).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# 3. Create your first roadmap
<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Go to Roadmaps and click create button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Write your view name, select the item type and choose your column and swimlane property.
  </Step>
</Steps>
